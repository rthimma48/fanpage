Biography

So, let's get started! First of all, who is NewJeans? Newjeans are a K-pop girl group who were formed by ADOR and HYBE labels. They debuted in 2022 and became popular in South Korea and overseas because of their influence with different generations, mostly Gen Z. NewJeans has five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein and they also have a fanbase who are called Bunnies. They went viral with two of their songs OMG and Ditto because of their connected stories and feelings of nostalgia. NewJeans mostly have around 3 comebacks per year but as of 2025 they are on hiatus due to company problems.
